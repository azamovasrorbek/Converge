Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats do you support? ↓

We support JPG, JPEG, PNG, BMP, and WebP. For iPhone users, we recommend converting HEIC to JPG before upload for the best results.

Is there a file limit? ↓

You can synthesize up to 50 images into a single PDF per session to maintain peak processing speed.

How is my privacy handled? ↓

Unlike other services, your photos are processed in the browser’s temporary memory. We never store your data on our servers.

Will the PDF quality be high? ↓

Yes. We use high-resolution rendering to ensure your text and images remain sharp for both digital viewing and printing.

Is this service mobile-friendly? ↓

Absolutely. Converge is designed with a responsive UI that works perfectly on iOS and Android browsers.

Do I need to install any software? ↓

No. Converge is a 100% web-based tool. You only need a modern browser and an internet connection.
